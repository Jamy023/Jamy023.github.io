B1 Level Short Articles Practice

1. My Daily Routine

Task: Write about your typical day, including your activities and schedule.

Useful Expressions: - First, I usually... - Then, I... - After that... - Finally... - In the evening...

Word count: 80-100 words

Words: 0

2. My Last Weekend

Task: Write about what you did last weekend. Use past tense verbs.

Remember to use: - Past tense verbs (went, visited, watched) - Time expressions (last weekend, on Saturday, in the morning) - Connecting words (and, but, because)

Word count: 70-90 words

Words: 0

3. My Favorite Place

Task: Describe your favorite room, park, or place in your city.

Include: - Where it is - What it looks like - Why you like it - What you do there

Word count: 80-100 words

4. My Best Friend

Task: Write about your best friend or a family member.

Tell us about: - Their appearance - Their personality - What you like to do together - Why they are special to you

Word count: 70-90 words

5. My Favorite Food

Task: Write about your favorite meal and how to prepare it.

Talk about: - What the food is - What ingredients you need - How to make it - Why you like it
