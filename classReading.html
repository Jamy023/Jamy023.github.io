My Classmates

Hi! My name is Sarah, and I want to tell you about my classmates. We are a small group of ten students in our English class. Each of us has different interests and hobbies.

First, there is John. He is 26 years old and loves playing soccer. He plays every weekend with his friends. John is very friendly and always makes us laugh.

Then, we have Maria. She is 24 years old and enjoys painting. She often brings her sketches to class. Maria is very creative and has a great imagination.

Next is Ahmed. He is 23 years old and loves technology. He wants to be a software engineer. Ahmed is very smart and helps us with our homework.

We also have Lisa. She is 25 years old and loves to dance. She takes dance classes every week. Lisa is very energetic and always encourages us to join her.

Another classmate is David. He is 27 years old and enjoys cooking. He often shares his recipes with us. David is a great cook, and we love to try his food!

Finally, there is Emily. She is 22 years old and loves reading books. She always has a book with her. Emily is very quiet but has a lot of interesting stories to share.

We all get along very well. After class, we often go to a café together to study or just talk. I am happy to have such wonderful classmates!
