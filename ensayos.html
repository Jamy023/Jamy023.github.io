Basic Essay Writing Practice

1. Writing an Introduction

Topic: Write an introduction paragraph about your favorite hobby.

Requirements:

  • Include a hook sentence
  • Provide background information
  • End with a thesis statement

2. Supporting Paragraph Development

Topic: Write a paragraph explaining the advantages of learning a new language.

Requirements:

  • Start with a clear topic sentence
  • Include at least two specific examples
  • Use transition words

3. Opinion Expression

Topic: Write a paragraph about whether social media has a positive or negative impact on society.

Requirements:

  • State your opinion clearly
  • Provide reasons for your opinion
  • Use appropriate opinion expressions (I believe, In my opinion, etc.)

4. Conclusion Writing

Topic: Write a conclusion paragraph for an essay about the importance of exercise.

Requirements:

  • Restate the main idea
  • Summarize key points
  • End with a final thought or call to action

5. Complete Mini Essay

Topic: Write a short essay about your dream job.

Requirements:

  • Include an introduction, body paragraph, and conclusion
  • Use appropriate transitions between paragraphs
  • Include specific examples and details
