English Reading Practice - Level A1

My Day

Hello! My name is Tom. I am 20 years old. I live in London. I am a student. I study English at a language school.

Every day, I wake up at 7:00 AM. I take a shower and get dressed. Then I eat breakfast. I like toast and coffee for breakfast.

I go to school at 8:30 AM. I walk to the bus stop and take bus number 42. My classes start at 9:00 AM and finish at 3:00 PM.

After school, I go to a café with my friends. We drink coffee and practice English together. Sometimes we go to the park if the weather is nice.

I go home at 6:00 PM. I eat dinner with my host family. After dinner, I do my homework. I watch TV or read a book before bed. I go to sleep at 11:00 PM.

On weekends, I visit museums or go shopping. I love living in London!
