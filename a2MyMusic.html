Hi! My name is Sarah. I am 25 years old. I love music! My favorite genres are pop, rock, and jazz.

In the morning, I usually listen to pop music. It makes me feel happy and energetic. My favorite pop singer is Taylor Swift.

In the afternoon, I like to listen to rock music. I enjoy bands like Coldplay and Imagine Dragons. Their songs are very powerful.

In the evening, I relax with some jazz music. I love the smooth sounds of the saxophone. My favorite jazz musician is Miles Davis.

On weekends, I often go to concerts with my friends. We have a great time enjoying live music together. Music is a big part of my life!
