English Reading Practice - Level A2

My Weekend

Hi! My name is Tom. I am 22 years old. I live in London. I study English at a language school. On weekends, I like to relax and have fun.

On Saturday, I usually sleep late. I wake up at 9:00 AM. After breakfast, I go to the park with my friends. We play football or go for a walk.

In the afternoon, I sometimes visit my family. We have lunch together and talk about our week. I enjoy spending time with them.

On Sunday, I like to do my homework in the morning. I study for my English classes. In the afternoon, I watch movies or read books.

In the evening, I prepare for the next week. I organize my school things and go to bed early. I love my weekends!
