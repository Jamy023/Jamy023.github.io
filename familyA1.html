English Reading Practice - Level A1

My Family

Hello! My name is Tom. I want to tell you about my family. I have a small family. There are four people in my family: my father, my mother, my sister, and me.

My father's name is John. He is 45 years old. He is a doctor. He works in a hospital. My father is very kind and helps many people.

My mother's name is Mary. She is 43 years old. She is a teacher. She teaches English at a school. My mother loves reading books and cooking delicious food.

I have one sister. Her name is Lucy. She is 10 years old. She is a student. Lucy loves playing with her friends and drawing pictures. She is very creative.

We live in a small house in the city. We have a garden with flowers. On weekends, we like to spend time together. We go to the park, have picnics, and play games.

I love my family very much. They make me happy!
